RuneWars: How to Play

The Battlefield Awaits!

RuneWars is a strategic turn-based RPG where two teams clash. Characters act in sequence, determined by their speed. Lead your team to victory by outmaneuvering your opponents!

Characters will have turn order on basis of their speed stats.

Master Your Skills & Skill Points

  • Skill Points (SP): A shared team resource, capped at 5.

  • Basic Attack (ATK): Deals damage, and earns your team +1 SP.

  • Skill: Unique ability, consumes -1 SP

  • Note: If SP is 0, characters must use a basic attack to gain SP before using a skill.

Understanding Character Status

Keep an eye on these visual cues during battle:

  • Yellow Border: This indicates the character currently selected by the player.

  • Red Border: This highlights the enemy character that is targeted for an attack.

  • Blue Border: Shows the Sheild is active on the characters.

  • Green Bar: Shows the character's current Health Points (HP).
